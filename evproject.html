EV Charging Station Project

As part of the Heath IBEP Program at UT, I was placed on a team of business and engineering students and we worked together to assess the current and future demand of electric vehicles on campus. We then developed a scalable system that could be used to determine when and where to install new chargers. As an electrical engineer, I had a great opportunity to learn about power systems and to speak with several professors and researchers to determine the logistical possibility of this project.
