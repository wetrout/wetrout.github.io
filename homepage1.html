Website Goals

Right now, this website is very basic. It would look a lot better if I used a website editor like Squarespace to put it together rather than write it myself from scratch using html, CSS, and Javascript. But nevertheless, here I am. I have my LinkedIn profile linked in (shameless pun) the contact information section below if you want to get a better picture of my resume and see my accomplishments. I will be working on this with spare time that I have during the fall semester of 2019, and I hope to have a site that I am happy with and that's beginning to look professional by the time Christmas rolls around this year. Of course, I will always continue to add to it and improve features. My goal is to have this homepage with links to my resume, pages for all the college projects I have done, pages for my personal projects, and a blog that I will regularly use to share my progress on these projects and which will autonomously add these updates to posts my LinkedIn profile. I plan to keep track of major updates by maintaining an "about my website" page where I will link .html files of each version of my site that I upload as I go along so a user can track my progress.
