Eagle Scout Project

For my Eagle Scout project, my troop and I expanded a small greenhouse at a local Boys and Girls Club to twice its original size, built a large work table, replaced the greenhouse cloth, plastic, and fan, built a door, and added mulch around the structure to allow for better landscaping.


In Boy Scouts I completed 20+ merit badges that covered topics from first aid to robotics. This project was a good test of everything I'd learned and a great way to finish my career in Boy Scouts.
