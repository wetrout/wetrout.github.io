English 298 (Chancellor's Honors English II) Project

The Assignment

The intention of this class was to teach us to do human subjects research according to IRB standards for our Chancellor's Honors Program Senior research projects.

Paper 1

For my paper in this class, I chose to research biometric technology used by cyclists. I created a poster to present these findings to my class.

*Poster QR code links here.

Paper 2

For the second paper in this class, I expanded on my research from Paper 1. I gathered more data and studied some of the potential drawbacks and hazards of using biometric fitness trackers.

What I Learned

We had to fill out a mock IRB proposal for our professor when we began working on the project. I learned how to perform ethical human subjects research and compile interview data into an accurate, concise sheet of usable quotes.

I had just spent 2/3 of the semester studying this subject, but I was able to practice taking a new approach to an idea in research; as obvious as it might seem, I had not even considered researching the drawbacks of using biometrics.
