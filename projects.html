EF 157/158 Projects
In these first-year engineering classes I was introduced to the engineering design process and completed several projects to learn how to apply these principles.
Arduino-Controlled Manufacturing Arm
As part of a team, I added an Arduino Uno to an already-assembled robot arm and wrote a program in Matlab that picked up an object (from an assembly line), collected data about the object, and decided whether it should continue in an assembly line or be discarded.
Cycling Biometrics Research Project
I interviewed several cyclists and performed research to analyze the benefits of using biometric fitness-tracking technology while cycling.
Eagle Scout Project
I built a greenhouse for a local Boys and Girls Club for my Eagle Scout project. I picked up many construction skills along the way.
EV Charging Station Project
I helped my team of interdisciplinary students assess the University's electric vehicle charging capacity and develop a system to choose new spaces.
